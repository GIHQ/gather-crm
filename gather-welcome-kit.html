Quick Answers
?

Who can see what?

Right now, only team members with @goldininstitute.org accounts can log in and access full features. When we invite fellows, they'll have more limited access ‚Äî they can view the directory and edit their own profiles, but won't see staff notes or admin tools.

?

Is the data real?

Yes! All 292 fellow records with photos, program info, and focus areas are real and imported from our existing database. Interactions you log are real too, so feel free to record genuine touchpoints.

?

What's coming next?

We're building a Community tab with announcements, a resource library, and a newsletter tool. We're also adding better search, cohort messaging, and more notification options.

?

Something's broken ‚Äî what do I do?

Use the suggestion box above! Select "Bug Report" and describe what happened. Screenshots are incredibly helpful. Don't worry about breaking anything ‚Äî that's what testing is for.
